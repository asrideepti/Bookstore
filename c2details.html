Name of the book : Comedy Of Doom
Author : Joseph Scrishaw

A collection of essays, parodies, and true stories covering every major topic in geek culture.



Description:
Joseph Scrimshaw is an American comedian and writer for radio, television and theater, whom City Pages, a Twin Cities newspaper, describes as having an "irreverent and insightful wit." He is the author of a book of comic essays called Comedy of Doom and hosts a monthly podcast called Obsessed with Joseph Scrimshaw, where he and his guests discuss their obsessions
