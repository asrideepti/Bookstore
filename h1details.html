Name of the book : The Walking Dead
Author : Robert Kirkman and Jay Bonansinga

Fight the dead. Fear the living.



Description:
The novel is a spin-off of Kirkman's series of graphic novels and explores the back-story of one of the series' most infamous characters, Lilly Caul. The Road to Woodbury is the second in a trilogy of novels,[1] following The Walking Dead: Rise of the Governor and preceding The Walking Dead: The Fall of the Governor.
