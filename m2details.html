Name of the book : Hanuman
Author : Maya Dayal



Description:
This book presents everyone’s favourite Monkey God, in a delightful avatar. Beautiful illustrations, exciting games and activities and a fun and engaging narration add to the appeal of this retelling of Hanuman’s life.
