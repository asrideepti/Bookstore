Name of the book : The Complete World Of Greek Mythology
Author : Richard Buxton

Greek myths are among the most complex and influential stories ever told.



Description:
This book combines a retelling of Greek myths with a comprehensive account of the world in which they developed their themes, their relevance to Greek religion and society, and their relationship to the landscape. "Contexts, Sources, Meanings" describes the main literary and artistic sources for Greek myths, and their contexts, such as ritual and theater.
